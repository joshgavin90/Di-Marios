Welcome to Di Marios!

About us:

- Our facilities Off-street parking, Wheelchair Accessability, Full licence for wine and beer, Takeaway and delivery, Gluten free pasta and pizza, Gluten free beers, Parties catered for, Baby Changing Area, High Chairs, Free WiFi.

- All our pasta, pizza dough and sauces are freshly home made here. If you have special dietary requirements you can still enjoy those Italian classics with our Gluten free pasta and pizzas.

- Di Marios Italian Restaurant provides a relaxed family friendly environment in which to enjoy authentic Italian cooking.

- Whether you are celebrating, a family get-together, communion, confirmation, a treat night out or a romantic dinner for two we would be delighted to welcome you to Da Mario.

- Our opening hours Monday to Saturday; 3pm to 10:30pm and Sunday; 4pm to 10pm

We open our first restaurant on January 1, 2020. Georges Street Arcade, South Great George's Street, Dublin. Di Marios provides a friendly and convenient location to enjoy a family event, a work gathering or just a relaxed night out. If you are driving we have ample off street parking at the rear of the restaurant.
